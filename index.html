Our Story

Our story is a little different. It did not begin with love. It began with friendship—quiet, ordinary, and therefore rare. ... On January 5th, 2022, a small Facebook message appeared on a screen. Nothing dramatic, no promises, no poetry. Just a simple "Hii" that somehow decided to stay. I don’t know how, but today—four years later—time has slipped past us, gently, without asking permission.

Those four years carried many moments. Ordinary days that later learned how to matter.Every day we went to Chawkbazar, especially to Nijam Sir’s English coaching. Sometimes I think I went there less for English and more for you.

Studying happened—here and there—almost as an optional subject. What truly mattered was the waiting.After coaching ended, I would stand downstairs, looking at the stairs, waiting for the moment you’d appear, step by step. That waiting became a habit. Almost a ritual.

Nearly one whole year passed like that—friendship growing quietly, comfortably.We invented reasons to see each other. I even attended exams for subjects you hated—Bangla, the worst of them all—just to meet once. We talked standing in the staircase room, stealing moments from time.

And time, mischievous as ever, kept running away. In the blink of an eye, we reached the HSC exams.After that, meetings became rare. Life pulled us into different directions. But distance found no victory—because hours on the phone replaced footsteps, and endless chats became part of our daily routine.

Somewhere along the way, without realizing it, we became each other’s best friend.Yet I stayed silent. I didn’t have the courage. Not then. Not even when my heart already knew the truth. I couldn’t tell you how I felt. I couldn’t give words to what lived inside me.

Until time, at last, opened a door. And I stepped through it.I finally said it: I love you. I have feelings for you. I want a future with you.From that moment to today, we have been together—walking forward, side by side.

This is my story. One day, I will sit quietly and listen as you tell me yours.
